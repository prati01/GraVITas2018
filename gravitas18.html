About

graVITas is VIT University's annual knowledge carnival which aims to empower the young minds by giving them a national platform to showcase their genius and innovation. The Technical festival is spread over 3 days with over 100 events, 50 workshops with over 30,000 footfalls. Started in 2009 by a group of enthusiastic VITians, graVITas now in its 10th edition has become the largest technical festival of its kind. Each year graVITas bring in a new theme, thus providing the eager participants a new experience every time.
